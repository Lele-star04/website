About Olly Motors

At Olly Motors Garage, we've been keeping our community on the road since day one. We're a family-owned workshop built on honest work, fair prices, and treating every car — and every customer — like our own.

From routine services and brakes to engine diagnostics and major repairs, our certified mechanics have the tools and experience to get the job done right the first time. No jargon, no upsells — just clear advice and reliable workmanship.

Whether you drive a daily commuter, a bakkie, or a family SUV, you can count on us to be straight with you and get you back on the road safely.
